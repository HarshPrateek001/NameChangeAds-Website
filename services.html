Affidavit Drafting for Name Change

Affidavit aapke name change process ka base document hai. Agar yahi galat hua to aage sab jagah problem aa sakti hai.

Hum aapka affidavit state rules ke hisaab se draft karte hain, jisme aapka old name, new name, address, reason aur declaration clearly likha hota hai. Ye affidavit notary ya magistrate ke saamne sign karaya jata hai.

  • Stamp paper value & wording as per state requirement.
  • Correct format for adults, minors, married persons, etc.
  • Sample affidavit shared in advance for your review.

Affidavit sahi hone se aapka newspaper ad aur Gazette form easily accept ho jata hai. Galat format ka affidavit kai baar reject bhi ho jata hai.

Affidavit

What we need from you

Share the following so we can prepare your affidavit exactly as required by government rules.

  • Current full name & proposed new name
  • Date of birth & full postal address
  • Reason for name change
  • Copy of any one ID proof
What you get
  • Professionally drafted affidavit text
  • Guidance for e-stamp / stamp paper purchase

Newspaper Advertisement for Name Change

Kai states me naam badalne ke liye 1 English + 1 regional language newspaper me ad dena zaroori hota hai.

Hum aapka ad matter bilkul sahi legal format mein banate hain jisme aapka old name, new name, address, date of affidavit, aur relevant details mention hoti hain. Fir hum suitable newspaper select karke aapka ad publish karwate hain.

  • English + regional (Marathi, Hindi, etc.) newspaper options.
  • Weekend editions / higher visibility placement suggestions.
  • Original newspaper copies or e-paper proof for Gazette use.

Galat format ya incomplete ad kabhi-kabhi department accept nahi karta. Isi liye hum ready-made legally acceptable format use karte hain.

Sample Ad Format

Legal ad copy template commonly used for Gazette submissions.

I, <Old Name>, residing at <Address>, have changed my name to <New Name> vide affidavit dated <Date>. Hereafter, I shall be known as <New Name> for all purposes.

Supported Publications
  • • Leading English dailies (city / state specific)
  • • State language newspapers (e.g., Marathi in Maharashtra)
  • • E-paper edition where acceptable

Gazette Notification – Legal Proof of Name Change

Gazette me aapka naam publish hona sabse strong legal proof hota hai. Banks, passport office, aur government departments isko officially accept karte hain.

Hum aapke state ke rules ke hisaab se forms fill karte hain, sahi documents attach karte hain, fees check karte hain aur application submit karne mein help karte hain. Kuch cases me courier / offline submission bhi hota hai jisme bhi guidance di jati hai.

  • Form filling & document checklist.
  • Correct photo, affidavit, newspaper ad attachment.
  • Follow-up on expected publication date.

Gazette publish hone ke baad aap future me apne sabhi records – Aadhaar, PAN, passport, bank, education certificates – me naam update kar sakte hain.
