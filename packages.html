Simple Steps: Name Change Ad in Bangalore Newspapers

If you need to publish a name change ad in Bangalore newspapers, here’s a simple guide to help you through the process.

  1. Choose your newspapers:

    For most passport and legal purposes, you should publish in two newspapers:

    • One English newspaper (like The Times of India or Deccan Herald)
    • One Kannada newspaper (like Vijay Karnataka or Prajavani)
  2. Prepare Your Details:

    Prepare your Name Change Ad using this format. Keep these details ready (they should match your affidavit / ID):

    • Old full name & New name
    • Father’s / spouse’s name
    • Age and complete Bangalore address
    • Affidavit number/date & Notary name (if available)
  3. Upload documents and make payment:

    You’ll need to provide: Self-attested photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.) and Payment for the ad.

  4. Choose a Publishing Date:

    Select your preferred publication date. Next Day is available if you submit before 3:00 pm.

  5. Confirm Your Booking:

    You’ll get a confirmation email and a reminder 24 hours before the ad is published.

  6. Check the Publication:

    Once published, check for accuracy. If there’s a mistake, we’ll re-publish it free of charge.

  7. Collect Your Copies:

    You’ll get a soft copy via email. You can also buy printed copies from your local vendor.

When is a Name Change Ad in Bangalore needed?

Publishing Name Change advertisements in two newspapers (1 national and 1 local) is a formality for applying for passport and other legal purposes in the following circumstances:

  • Change of one’s own name / father’s name
  • Change of religion from X to Y
  • Change in Spelling
  • Changing long names to short
  • Changing your surname
  • Name change ads in Bangalore / Bengaluru after marriage
  • Name in birth certificate does not match with the leaving certificate

Note: Deed Poll Affidavit / Annexure E required earlier at the Passport Office for Change of Name Ads has been DISCONTINUED. Now, only a self-attested Photo Identity proof with the correct name, along with a newspaper ad in 1 local and 1 National Newspaper, is required!
